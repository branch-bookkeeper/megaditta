Branch Bookkeeper

Nowadays working on a crowded git repository can easily be a nightmare in which you can't merge your PR on master.

You rebase your branch and wait for the (slow) CI checks to finish. Just to find out that somebody merged and you've to do everything again. Over and over.

Branch Bookkeeper is a simple queue management system that will keep track of merge order for each PR and destination branch.

You book your place with a simple click and just wait your turn. Nobody will steal your place again, just click and forget. You'll be notified as soon as your turn arrives. You rebase and you'll be notified again after CI completes its checks and you're ready to merge.

Stop staring at your PR page!

Installation is simple too! You can quickly deploy it on Heroku, Docker Hub or wherever you can get Node.js running. Just fill some details and you're ready to go!
